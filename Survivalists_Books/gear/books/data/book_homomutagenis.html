Homo Mutagenis or "Mutants" are a unique branch of humanity owing to their remarkable potential
in that they are able to adapt to irradiated environments and consume irradiated species of animals and plant life,
they are also able to consume human flesh without contracting the Kuru disease.

A common misconception that mutants face is that they all "must" consume human flesh to survive, this is false
with appropriate support networks and the necessary resources, mutants can survive and thrive without needing to consume human biomatter,
The flowers that can be found across Deer Isles enable for these alternative resources to be found.

Mutants can trace their altered genetic lineage to a singular source, that being an individual going by the name of Warlock,
a man who can be considered the progenitor of the mutant subspecies and their feral counterparts.

Mutant physiology has the potential to adapt with appropriate resources, these can grant individuals with specific abilities,
ranging from inbuilt night-vision to the ability to consume human food again which they would otherwise be unable to do

Homo Mutangenis & Mutagenis Caput Cancri aka "headcrabs"
Mutagenis Caput Cancri or "headcrabs" as they are better known reside inside of the biozones that
dot area's of Deer Isles, they are essentially walking grenades.
Headcrabs when expended are able to be skinned and consumed by mutated individuals.

Homo Mutagenis & Feral Alpha Mutants
1. Feral alpha mutants are non-sentient and will attack any who approach a nests proximity.
2. When an feral alpha mutant is defeated, they are able to be skinned and the meat also consumable by mutants.
3. Feral alpha nests contain eggs, these eggs enable a mutant to adapt their bodies to suit their needs.

Homo Mutagenis and Mutated Wolves.
Mutated Wolves are a varation of the common wolf species
the meat that these wolves are hunted for are consumable by mutants.
Any pelts that are not to badly damaged can be used in leather based products
