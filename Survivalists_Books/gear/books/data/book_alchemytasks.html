Each level in the Society requires certain tasks. These tasks teach you the craft. As you progress through the
tasks, you will advance in the Socity.

Alchemy Societ - Task 1 - Apprentice - The First Task
So you want to be an Alchemist? Your first task will prove your dedication and show you the work involved
in becoming an Alchemist.
Find, buy or acquire a Mortar and Pestle
Grow, dry and grind two full piles of all eight herbs. Brookmint, Dock, Green Aminita, Mint, Ribwort, Rosemary
Valarian and Yarrow.
Bring the herbs to an Adept Alchemist for your next tasks and entry into the Society.

Alchemy Society - Task 3 - Novice - The Discovery Process
Each recipe has three possible herbs that will work in each of the five slots. Find all the herbs for each slot.
Fill the mortar with a known recipe.
Replace each herb in slot one with a new herb. Use the rags to test the combination. It will indicate a valid
recipe.
Record your findings without completing the mixing process.
Repeat for each slot to find all the possibilities for that recipe.

Alchemy Society - Task 4 - Novice - Finding the Optimal Recipe
1. Load the mortar with a known recipe with full piles of herbs.
2. Use the reagent to make the recipe and record how much of each herb was used.
3. Reload the mortar with the next unique recipe, making sure each slot has a new herb.
4. Make the recipe and record the results.
5. Repeat one more time. Study your notes to see which herb uses the least amount for each of the slots.
That is your optimal recipe.

Alchemy Society - Task 5 - Novice - Finding New Recipes
Discover at least three optimal medicine and two dye recipes using trail and error or through lessons with your
teacher, or networking with your peers.
The reagent for meds, dyes, drinks, stim packs, test kits and face paint is the lab tube.

Alchemy Society - Task 6 - Scholar - Learning All the Recipes
Learn at least one recipe for all nine medicines, all seven dyes, all five drinks, both tesk kits, the face
paint and the crude stim pack.

Alchemy Society - Task 7 - Scholar - Learnig Every Possible Recipe
Using your discovery skills, determine all valid herbs for all slots for all recipes of the craft.

Alchemy Society - Task 8 - Scholar - Optimal Achievement
Find the optimal recipes for all recipes.

Alchemy Society - Task 9 - Adept - Teach Others
Use your knowledge of the craft to teach others how to follow the path. Let them find how the seed becomes
