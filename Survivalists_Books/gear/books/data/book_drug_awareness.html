Nicotine


Cigarette's and Cigar's nowadays are rolled with refined tobacco; no extra additives. A finely rolled cigar commands attention and bolsters charisma. Some will notice a slight dry mouth along with an adreneline rush. This extra flow of blood allows for dilated pupils which makes the world seem more vibrant. As one of the lesser narcotics, the dependency is moderate as poses less risk than zeds.


Tetrahydrocannabinol (THC)


Arguably one of the most dangerous narcotics. Legends are told of a madness that manifests from the onset of your first puff. A sweet smelling joint is a trap beckoning weak willed individuals. Those enthralled by the madness report cotton mouth and ravenous appetites. The world becomes vibrant beyond compare and some are said to lose their minds in the beauty. Use at your own risk, lest the madness root itself in you.


Methamphetamine


Originally developed as an anti-depressant and diet medication, the mood-elevating effect allowed for extensive use in millitary application. At the onset of the apocalypse, the only way to combat the walking dead was to never let your guard down. Without proper manufacturing facilities and tools, post apocalyptic meth is riddled with many deterrents. Long term users report blurred vision, spontaneous bleeding, vomiting, auditory hallucinations, and the ability to fight like an animal.


Bath Salts


In the peak of civilization pre apocalypse, we had the luxury of bathing without worry. These baths were magnificent and moreso if salts were dissolved into the water. Turns out these salts also pack a punch when ingested. Sometimes you want to eat the face off an enemy while bleeding from your eyes and I can't stop you. Side effects are vomiting, bleeding from the ears, blurred vision, auditory hallucinations, loss of color contrast, and a familiar urge to rage.


Lysergic Acid Diethylamide (LSD)


A mainly hallucinogenic drug with symptoms that includes visual and auditory hallucinations. The onset is quick and lasts for a significant amount of time. There is a chance that a bad trip will never let you return to normal; beware.
