Tomato sauce:
Crush de tomato in de big metal round for cook e hot with lot of de tomato.
Take de spice e put in de metal ting . Let less water e mix sometime until e ready.

Pizza:
Make de nest of flour on cut plank e put de acqua e il uovo al centro de la farina.
Mescolarli togeder e put bit of yeast in de pasta. Make turn de pasta for forma la pizza. Put de tomato sauce e de fromaggio on de pizza.

Pasta dish:
Make de nest of flour on cut plank e put de acqua e il uovo al centro de la farina.
Mescolarli togeder e put bit of yeast in de pasta.
Roll, cut e flat de pasta for form you want. De hammer o tenderizer e good for dis.
Put de tomato sauce on de pasta dish.
